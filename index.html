About

Terper is a multidisciplinary duo based in Barcelona.

Formed by Paola Idrontino and RDMT, our sound reflects the creative heritage distilled by our respective creative paths in the fields of textile craftsmanship and audiovisual creation, embracing poetic passion and multi-instrumentalism in pursuit of elevating each other and our listeners.

Our work has been exhibited in MACBA, Ket Galeria, Centre d'Artesania de Catalunya, Konvent Zero, Espai Niu, Sala VOL, Centre Civico de Barceloneta, Zilzal Sonic Ceremony, & A Love Supreme Festival.
