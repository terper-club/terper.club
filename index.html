About

Sound that weaves.

We create multidisciplinary art installations combining audiovisual work, textile crafting, and music production. Each piece explores the intersection of spoken word, experimental beats, and tactile creation.

Our work lives in galleries, performance spaces, and anywhere the boundaries between mediums dissolve.

Based in Barcelona, creating between poetry and percussion.
