Breathing Waves

Museu d'Art Contemporani de Barcelona (MACBA)

A dance that represents the relationship between humans and nature.

"I will always be here: a place you belong”, speaks the Ocean.

Reminding us that we breathe the ocean. The ocean is inside of us. It gives us life. The dance holds our hand from the shore to the deepest depths, where we are invited to marvel at the ocean’s beauty, but also to realise the devastating effects that climate change, pollution, and destructive fishing practices have caused on our essential ecosystem.

"I will always be here" is the last whisper. There is forgiveness. The Ocean will survive us and thrive.

The ocean is where we came from… and where we are going…
